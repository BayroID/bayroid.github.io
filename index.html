ABOUT

BayroID is a digital identity solution that allows users to control their data and identity across the digital world.

BayroID aims to create a more private, secure and interoperable online identity experience.

BayroID is based on cutting-edge technologies such as blockchain, decentralized identifiers, verifiable credentials and ZK Proof. This allows users to own and manage their own data, decide what information they share and with whom they share it, and access online services without having to reveal more personal data than necessary.
